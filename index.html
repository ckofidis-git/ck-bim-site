About

Meet Christos Kofidis.

Christos is a Chartered Construction Manager (MCIOB) with an MEng in Civil & Structural Engineering, and has spent 15+ years making BIM and digital construction actually work — not in slide decks, but on live mega-programmes across the UK, the Middle East and Greece.

His career has run through aviation megaprojects, national high-speed rail, nuclear new-build, water infrastructure and next-generation city development. Different sectors, same mission: get the right information, to the right people, in a form they can act on.

He believes the best information management is invisible — teams just feel that coordination is smoother, reviews are faster and nothing falls through the cracks. Getting there takes strategy, standards and a healthy obsession with automating everything that doesn't need a human.

MCIOB · Chartered Construction Manager MEng Civil & Structural Engineering Named in a national “Top 50 BIM Managers” list, 2025

A note on confidentiality

The programmes Christos has worked on are public record — but that's where it ends. Playbook

Signature moves

Real results from real programmes — anonymised, because that's how he works.

01

Automate the boring

Designed a review-distribution engine connecting forms, email, task boards and BI dashboards for 40+ reviewers across 7 disciplines. Zero spreadsheets harmed; over $100k saved every year.

02

Validate at scale

Built self-serve web apps that automatically check model element attributes against the project's component catalogue — saving £200k+ in man-hours and making quantity takeoff data trustworthy.

03

Make the model the meeting room

Pioneered cloud federated-model adoption across an entire programme, setting the standard others followed. The models he managed consistently ranked highest in active users.

04

Give data a pulse

Created automated flows that extract live model registers straight from the CDE and share them with every task team weekly. It became the programme-wide standard.
